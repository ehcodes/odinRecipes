Broccoli Cheese Bites

Description

Are you looking for an easy healthy snack? These Broccoli Cheese Bites are just what you need! Making these savory snacks is quick and easy.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 oz cream cheese
  • 3 cups lightly steamed broccoli
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • salt and pepper
  • 3 tbsp coconut flour
  • Cooking Spray

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In a medium sized mixing bowl, combine the cheddar cheese, cream cheese and eggs.
  3. Mix the ingredients currently in the mixing bowl and the stir in the broccoli.
  4. Gently fold in the baking powder, coconut flour, salt and pepper.
  5. Chill the dough for 10 minutes, this will allow the flour to absorb the moisture.
  6. Lightly spray a standard 12 count muffin pan with cooking spray.
  7. Spoon the dough into the pan.
  8. Place the pan in the oven and bake for 15-17 minutes.
