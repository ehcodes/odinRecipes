Peanut Butter Balls

Description

These peanut butter balls are easy to make and pleasing to the taste buds. Once you're done making your won, you'll be glad you gave them a shot!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted creamy peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup coconut flour
  • 3 tbsp maple syrup

Steps

  1. Line a baking sheet with wax or parchment and set it aside.
  2. In a medium mixing bowl, add the peanut butter, coconut flour, and maple syrup.
  3. Use a fork to stir the items in the bowl until well mixed.
  4. Still in the bowl, use a silicone spatula to fold until well incorporated and thickened.
  5. Using a small cookie scoop, scoop and drop peanut butter balls onto the prepared baking sheet. Make sure to space them evenly apart.
  6. The peanut butter balls are ready for consumption and can be stored via the fridge, in an airtight container.
